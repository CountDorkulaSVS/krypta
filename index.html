  • General

  • What is the Sneaky Vampire Syndicate?

    The Sneaky Vampire Syndicate is an NFT Collection of 8,888 Vampires living their best life in The Lair - away from any burning sunlight or pesky Vampire Hunters. The Syndicate is known for its community, eager to adopt their Sneaky Bats, play the SVS video game, enter The Lair (MetaVerse), and meet Female Vampires (Gen 2). All Vampire holders gain access to join their kin in the MetaVerse, while having access to their very own exclusive video game. 8,888 Vampires are now running wild, with their companions and much more coming soon.

  • What is an NFT?

    NFTs or "non-fungible-tokens" are unique, one of one, digital assests that are secured by a blockchain, like Ethereum. NFTs often take form of an image, video, or song, where the holder has full blockchain-proven ownership of the asset. NFTs have created a new medium for artists and collectors to share their pieces.

  • How many Vampires will there be?

    There will only ever be 8,888 (Gen1) Vampires minted for this collection. (Gen2) Female Vampires will have a collection size of 11,111. All Vampires have been designed by the great @migwashere who is the former artist of the BAYC.

  • How can I check the rarity of my Vampires?

    You can check the rarity of your Vampire on Rarity.Tools. Enter your Vampire's unique ID in the "ID Lookup" field and submit. You will be able to see your Vampire's rarity as well as the floor prices for your Vampire's traits.

  • What chain is the Sneaky Vampire Syndicate hosted on?

    SVS is an Ethereum-based NFT collection that can be bought and sold on the OpenSea NFT Marketplace using cryptocurrency (ETH) or NiftyGateway NFT Marketplace with fiat (USD).

  • Staking/$BLOOD Utility Token

  • What is Staking? What is the $BLOOD Utility Token?

    Staking is the act of sending your NFT assest to an "NFT Ecosystem" contract that provides safekeeping and governance. In most cases, Staking your asset(s) allows the owner to earn Ecosystem Utility Tokens.


    In the case of the Sneaky Vampire Syndicate, Staking your Vampires & Bats will allow owners to be rewarded with the Ecosystem's Utility Token known as the $BLOOD Token.

    The current staking rate is as follows: (3) $BLOOD Tokens per Vampire (Gen1) for every 24 hours it is Staked. (1) $BLOOD Token per Bat (Gen1) for every 24 hours it is Staked.

  • How do I Stake my Vampire for $BLOOD Tokens?

    In order to successfully Stake your SVS Vampire, follow the instructions below:


    1. Visit: https://dash.svs.gg/

    2. Connect your web3 wallet

    3. Click the "Approve Vampires" button

    4. Once the transaction is confirmed, click the "Stake" button.

    *Keep in mind there are a total of (2) transactions that must be completed in order to successfully Stake your Vampires*

  • Do I need to delist my Vampire from OpenSea before Staking?

    No, you will not need to delist your Vampire from OpenSea as the Staking process will take care of this for you.

  • What is the value of the $BLOOD Token?

    The SVS $BLOOD token has no intrinsic value as it is a Utility Token within the SVS NFT Ecosystem. (1) $BLOOD = (1) $BLOOD.

  • How will the $BLOOD Token be used in the SVS Ecosystem?

    The $BLOOD Token will be used throughout the SVS Ecosystem, including but not limited to:


    - Used to Mint Future NFTs or used as discounts for Minting.

    - Using $BLOOD in the MetaVerse to purchase exclusive items/perks.

    - Using $BLOOD to facilitate breeding between Vampires (Gen1) & Female Vampires (Gen2).

    - Using all of the royalties gained from the bats to purchase Vampires decided by the DAO and allowing you to swap $BLOOD for fractionalized tokens of those vampires, allowing you to own parts of rarer Vampires, such as gold ones, or even uniques.

    - Other uses to be annouced as the Project rolls through the road map!

  • My Vampire disappeared from my NFT wallet after Staking. What happened?

    When you Stake your Vampire, you are sending your Vampire from your wallet to the Staking Contract. You can think of the Staking Contract like a Bank Vault. Your Vampire will be safe and you will always remain the owner of the Vampire(s) you Staked.

  • Will I have to Unstake my Vampire(s) in order to claim their Companion Bats?

    As of this writing, NO you will not need to Unstake your Vampire(s) in order to claim your Companion Bats beginning October 21st, 2021.

  • Can I withdraw my Vampire from Staking anytime I want?

    Correct, there are no lock-ins, time minimums, or ties. You can stake and unstake your Vampires at will, any time.

  • I tried Staking but I don't think it is working?

    Please make sure you have gone through with both of the transactions, one of them is the approval for our contract to spend your tokens, the second one is the actual transfer of your tokens to the contract.

  • Game

  • What is the Game called and what are the requirements to play?

    The Game's name has yet to be determined as of this time and you must be a holder of at least (1) SVS NFT (Gen1, Gen2, Bats, etc) in order to play.

  • What style of Game is it and will there be Leaderboards?

    The game will be a singleplayer 2D side-climbing mashup between NES Castlevania and rogue-like, perma-death games such as Nuclear Throne / Enter the Gungeon / Spelunky.

    There will be two game modes at launch:
    ---> One being a "Random Seed Endless" mode that generates a new castle every time you start the game.
    ---> The other is a "Weekly Seed" chosen as the last block number of the last mined block @ Sunday 12 AM EST that generates the same castle for everyone. This one will have a score based leaderboard and the winner will receive bragging rights weekly (other rewards to be determined, such as airdrops or other rewards).

  • What's the game's backstory? What's the game about?

    Dracula's Castle, a once impenetrable fortress, has fallen; overtaken by vampire hunters and werewolves, Dracula is no where to be found. After hearing the news, you, a new Vampire Syndicate, has taken it upon yourself to approach the now dilapidated castle in an attempt to retake it for the Brood. What rests inside is unknown and the castle is ever-changing.
